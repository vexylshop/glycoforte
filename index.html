Who Should Avoid Glyco Forte?

Not recommended for pregnant or breastfeeding women and individuals with the following conditions:

  • Prostate enlargement
  • Ulcerative colitis
  • High blood pressure
  • Coronary artery disease
  • Hiatal hernia with reflux
  • Kidney or liver disease
